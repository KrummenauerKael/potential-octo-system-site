What you can do with CityEats

Friends Feed

See reviews from people you follow. Your feed shows what friends are eating around NYC.

Friends Map

View restaurants your friends have reviewed on an interactive map. Filter by specific friends.

Photo Reviews

Share your dining experiences with up to 6 photos per review. See real food photos from friends.

Restaurant Lists

Create themed lists like "Date Night Spots" or "Best Pizza". Share or keep them private.

Follow Friends

Connect with friends and see their restaurant activity. Get notified when they post.

Leaderboards

See who's reviewing the most and discover active community members.
