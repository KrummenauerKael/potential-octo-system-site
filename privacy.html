Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

What We Collect

  • Account info (email, username, profile photo)
  • Content you create (reviews, comments, lists, photos)
  • Location data (with your permission, to show nearby restaurants)

How We Use It

  • To provide and improve CityEats
  • To show you relevant content and restaurants
  • To send you notifications about activity on your posts

What Others See

Your profile, reviews, and public lists are visible to other CityEats users. You control your privacy through your account settings.

Data Storage

We use Firebase (Google) to securely store your data. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed immediately.

Your Choices

  • Update or delete your profile anytime
  • Control notification preferences in Settings
  • Delete your account and all data through Settings

Third-Party Services

CityEats uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, storage
  • Google Places API - Restaurant information
  • Google AdMob - Advertising (free tier only)
  • Apple Push Notifications - Notifications

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

CityEats is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at cityeatsappnyc@gmail.com
