Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data immediately.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots (•••) on any review or comment and select "Report". Our team reviews all reports.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link.

Is CityEats free?

Yes! CityEats is free to download and use. Premium subscription ($1.99/month) removes ads and unlocks unlimited lists.

Is there an Android version?

We're iOS-only for now. Android is planned for the future.

How do I become a beta tester?

Visit our beta page to join via TestFlight.

Bug Reports & Feedback

Found a bug or have a feature request? Email us at support@cityeatsapp.com with details about what happened and we'll look into it.
